================================================================================
                              WHAT IS DERMACLOUD?
================================================================================

DermaCloud is a cloud-based Hospital Management System (HMS) built specifically
for dermatology and cosmetology clinics. It combines AI-powered skin analysis,
complete patient management, pharmacy, billing, and clinic operations — all in
one platform designed for the modern Indian dermatologist.

No more juggling between multiple software tools. DermaCloud gives your entire
clinic — doctor, front desk, and pharmacy — one unified system.

================================================================================
                              WHO IS IT FOR?
================================================================================

  • Dermatologists running private clinics or small hospitals
  • Cosmetology & aesthetic medicine practices
  • Multi-specialty skin and hair clinics
  • Clinics with front desk staff and pharmacy counters

================================================================================
                           CORE FEATURES AT A GLANCE
================================================================================

1. AI-POWERED SKIN DIAGNOSIS
   ─────────────────────────
   • Upload dermoscope or clinical images directly from the consultation form
   • Our AI model (ViT — Vision Transformer) instantly analyzes the image and
     predicts the skin condition with confidence scores
   • Supports conditions: acne, eczema, psoriasis, vitiligo, fungal infections,
     melanoma, dermatitis, urticaria, and more
   • AI suggestions are shown to the doctor — the final diagnosis is always
     the doctor's decision (AI assists, never replaces)

2. PATIENT EXPLANATION GENERATOR (AI)
   ────────────────────────────────────
   • After every consultation, generate a personalized patient explanation
     in plain English using Claude AI (Anthropic)
   • Explains: what the condition is, why it happened, what each medicine does,
     and home care tips — in simple language the patient can understand
   • For multi-issue visits (e.g. acne + pigmentation), each condition gets its
     own dedicated explanation section
   • Supports Hindi and Kannada translations for regional patients

3. WHATSAPP REPORT DELIVERY
   ─────────────────────────
   • Send the PDF consultation report directly to the patient's WhatsApp
     with one click — no copy-pasting phone numbers
   • Uses Meta's official WhatsApp Business API for reliable delivery
   • Appointment confirmation messages are also sent via WhatsApp automatically

4. CONSULTATION MANAGEMENT
   ─────────────────────────
   • Separate consultation forms for Dermatology and Cosmetology
   • Multi-issue visit support: document up to 2 separate skin conditions
     in a single visit, each with their own images, AI analysis, diagnosis,
     and treatment plan
   • Fully customizable consultation forms — add, remove, or reorder fields
     to match your clinic's workflow
   • Consultation templates: save frequently used diagnoses and treatment
     plans as templates for faster documentation
   • Template search with real-time filtering inside dropdowns

5. CONSULTATION HISTORY & COMPARISON
   ────────────────────────────────────
   • Full consultation history for every patient across all visits
   • Cosmetology comparison view: compare before/after photos across visits
     side by side in a full-screen lightbox
   • For multi-issue visits, photos from each issue are clearly labeled
     and grouped separately in the comparison view
   • AI-generated patient summary cards for quick patient context

6. PDF REPORT GENERATION
   ───────────────────────
   • Generate professional, branded PDF reports for every consultation
   • Dermatology and Cosmetology reports with clinic letterhead
   • Includes: patient details, diagnosis, treatment plan, AI results,
     and doctor's notes
   • Share PDF via WhatsApp or download directly

7. PATIENT MANAGEMENT
   ───────────────────
   • Complete patient profiles: demographics, medical history, allergies,
     contact details
   • Unique Patient ID for every patient
   • Search patients instantly by name, phone, or patient ID
   • View complete consultation history for any patient in one place

8. APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING (FRONT DESK)
   ──────────────────────────────────────
   • Front desk staff can book, manage, and track appointments
   • Token number system for walk-in patients
   • Appointment statuses: Scheduled → Checked In → In Progress → Completed
   • Today's appointment overview on the dashboard

9. PHARMACY & INVENTORY
   ──────────────────────
   • Built-in pharmacy management for clinics with an in-house dispensary
   • Inventory tracking with low-stock and out-of-stock alerts
   • Sales billing with cash / card / UPI payment modes
   • Revenue tracking: daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports

10. ANALYTICS DASHBOARD
    ────────────────────
    • Real-time stats: total consultations, patients seen today, this month
    • Consultation trend charts: 7-day, 30-day, 90-day, 180-day views
    • Specialty split: dermatology vs. cosmetology breakdown
    • Top skin conditions treated (by frequency)
    • Patient demographics: age groups and gender distribution
    • Return patient rate and unique patient counts
    • Pharmacy revenue: total collected, pending dues, top-selling items
    • AI usage tracker: reports generated, translations, patient summaries

11. SUBSCRIPTION & BILLING
    ───────────────────────
    • Flexible subscription plans (Tier 1 and Tier 2)
    • Integrated Razorpay payment gateway for secure online payments
    • Google OAuth support for quick signup and login

================================================================================
                         SECURITY & DATA PROTECTION
================================================================================

  DermaCloud is built with security as a foundation, not an afterthought.

  ROLE-BASED ACCESS CONTROL
  • Doctors and front desk staff have completely separate access levels
  • Sensitive operations (creating consultations, AI analysis, clinic settings,
    templates, analytics) are restricted to the doctor's login only
  • Every API request is verified against the live database — stale or tampered
    tokens are rejected automatically

  AUTHENTICATION
  • Secure JWT-based authentication with 24-hour access tokens
  • Refresh tokens (30-day) for seamless re-authentication without repeated
    manual logins
  • Email OTP verification during signup — no unverified accounts
  • Separate token systems for doctors and front desk staff

  RATE LIMITING (BRUTE FORCE PROTECTION)
  • Login attempts are rate-limited: max 10 attempts per 15 minutes per user
  • OTP verification: max 10 attempts per 15 minutes
  • Signup: max 5 requests per hour
  • Password reset: max 5 requests per hour
  • Exceeding limits triggers a temporary lockout — protects against
    automated attack tools

  INPUT SANITIZATION
  • All user input is sanitized before being processed or stored
  • NoSQL injection attacks are blocked at the API layer
  • MongoDB query operators injected by attackers are automatically stripped
  • All database IDs are validated before any query is executed

  ACCOUNT LOCKOUT PROTECTION
  • After 5 consecutive failed login attempts, the account is automatically
    locked for 30 minutes — prevents password-guessing attacks
  • Applies to both doctor and front desk staff logins independently
  • The account holder receives an instant email security alert with the
    exact IP address that triggered the lockout and the unlock time
  • Accounts automatically unlock after 30 minutes — no manual intervention
    needed from support

  SECURITY HEADERS (BROWSER-LEVEL PROTECTION)
  • All pages and API responses include industry-standard HTTP security headers:
    – X-Frame-Options: DENY (prevents clickjacking attacks)
    – X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff (prevents MIME-type sniffing)
    – X-XSS-Protection: block (browser-level cross-site scripting guard)
    – Referrer-Policy: strict-origin (controls referrer information leakage)
    – Permissions-Policy: blocks camera, mic, geolocation by default
    – Strict-Transport-Security (HSTS): forces HTTPS for all connections
    – Content-Security-Policy: restricts where scripts, images, and
      fonts can be loaded from

  AUDIT LOGS (SECURITY TRAIL)
  • Every important action in your clinic is automatically recorded:
    – Doctor and front desk login attempts (success and failure)
    – Account lockout events with IP address
    – Every new consultation created
    – Every patient record created
    – Every template created or deleted
  • Logs are stored securely in your clinic's own database
  • Each log captures: who did it, when, from which IP, and what changed
  • Logs are automatically deleted after 90 days (DPDP compliance)
  • No extra cost — included in your existing plan, stored in MongoDB Atlas

  REFRESH TOKEN ROTATION
  • Every time a user silently re-authenticates (without logging in again), the
    old refresh token is immediately invalidated and replaced with a brand new one
  • If an attacker ever steals a refresh token, it becomes dead the moment the
    legitimate user next opens the app — automatic self-healing security
  • Logging out also invalidates all existing refresh tokens for that account

  LOGOUT TOKEN REVOCATION
  • When a user logs out, their access token is immediately blacklisted
    server-side — even if the token hasn't expired yet (24h window)
  • Blacklist entries auto-delete once the original token would have expired
    anyway (no permanent storage overhead)
  • Prevents session hijacking: a stolen token cannot be reused after logout

  PASSWORD STRENGTH ENFORCEMENT
  • Passwords are required to meet strict rules at signup and password change:
    minimum 8 characters, at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter,
    one number, and one special character
  • Enforced server-side — cannot be bypassed by modifying the app

  CORS LOCKDOWN
  • All API endpoints only accept requests from the official DermaCloud domain
  • Requests from unknown or unauthorized websites are automatically rejected
    with a 403 error — prevents third-party sites from silently calling
    your clinic's API using a logged-in session

  SENSITIVE DATA NEVER LEAKS INTO LOGS
  • Passwords, tokens, OTPs, and other sensitive fields are automatically
    stripped (replaced with [REDACTED]) before any data is written to logs
    or audit records
  • Prevents accidental exposure through log files or monitoring tools

  SECURE IMAGE STORAGE
  • Patient images (dermoscope, clinical photos) are stored on AWS S3
  • All image URLs are signed and expire after 1 hour — no permanent
    public links to sensitive medical images

  DATA OWNERSHIP
  • Each clinic's data is completely isolated — one clinic cannot access
    another clinic's patients, consultations, or records
  • All clinic ID checks happen server-side, not on the frontend

                         TECHNOLOGY STACK
================================================================================

  • Frontend:    Next.js 15 (React 19), Tailwind CSS
  • Backend:     Next.js API Routes (serverless)
  • Database:    MongoDB Atlas (cloud-hosted, replicated)
  • AI Engine:   Claude Sonnet (Anthropic) for patient explanations
  • AI Vision:   Custom ViT model for skin condition classification
  • Storage:     AWS S3 for medical images
  • WhatsApp:    Meta WhatsApp Cloud API (official Business API)
  • Payments:    Razorpay
  • Email:       Nodemailer (OTP and notifications)
  • Auth:        JWT + bcrypt + OTP
